Machineee

High-quality DIY projects with great assembly instructions.

Website, GitHub, Gitter, Twitter, Email, AngelList

Machineee organizes do-it-yourself electronics projects into complete kits with personalized step-by-step assembly instructions. We're doing this to make it possible for more individuals and communities to participate directly in technology innovation.

Our company is based on the premise that community-driven and personalized innovation will be increasingly important with continued trends toward deeper automation and on-demand manufacturing. We think DIY communities and enthusiasts will lead this transition. However, the impact they can have with current technology is limited because today's tools are too inefficient and not well adapted to the needs of DIY ecosystems.

The platform we're developing embeds automation techniques throughout DIY development workflows to accelerate them for current developers and simplify the way the tools are used to produce electronic systems and define their control software. This means more people can use them and participate in developing technology. We developed custom hardware for our platform that further has allowed us to greatly simplify system deployment with automation. Our hardware also provides a common backbone that can be used for many systems that exist today.

We're launching progressively over the course of this year. Reach out to us if you'd like to know more about our work.
